Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13.02.2026

LexaRank ("we", "our", or "us") operates the LexaRank mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

1. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you sign in using Google, Apple, email, or other authentication methods, we may collect:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile photo (if provided by login provider)

b) Guest Accounts

If you use the app without signing in, we may assign an anonymous identifier to store your progress locally or in our systems.

c) Usage Data

We collect gameplay progress, scores, league rankings, achievements, and in-app activity to improve user experience and ensure fair competition.

d) Purchases and Subscriptions

All payments, subscriptions, and in-app purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store credit card or payment details.

e) Advertising Data

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data in accordance with Google's Privacy Policy.

f) Notifications

We may send push notifications related to gameplay, achievements, promotions, or updates. Users may disable notifications in device settings.

g) Local Storage

We use local storage technologies (such as Hive and SharedPreferences) to store game progress and settings on your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • To process purchases and subscriptions

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with trusted third-party service providers strictly for operating the Service, including:

5. Data Retention

We retain user data for as long as necessary to provide the Service or comply with legal obligations. Users may request deletion of their account and associated data.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data. However, no system is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

LexaRank is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided personal information, we will delete such information promptly.
