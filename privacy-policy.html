Privacy Policy

LAST REVISED: FEBRUARY 13, 2026

At LexaRank, we care about your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you use our mobile application and related services (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

LexaRank collects information to provide a better gaming experience and ensure fair competition.

a) Account Registration

When you register, you may sign in using Google, Apple, or Email. We receive information from these providers such as your email address, name, and unique user ID. You may also use the Service as a Guest, in which case we assign a temporary identifier to your device.

b) Profile & Gameplay Data

Your public profile includes your username, ranking, league status, badges, and achievements. This information is visible to other users to facilitate the competitive nature of the Service. We store your scores, PvP match history, and progress in our secure Firebase database.

c) Technical Data & Local Storage

We use Hive and SharedPreferences for local data storage on your device to ensure fast access to your settings and game progress. We also collect IP addresses and device identifiers for security, fraud prevention, and analytics purposes.

2. How We Process Your Information

We process your data based on the following legal bases:

  • Providing the Service: Managing your account, PvP matches, and League standings.
  • Legitimate Interests: Improving game mechanics, performing research, and detecting cheating.
  • Personalized Advertising: Displaying relevant ads via Google AdMob.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only with trusted service providers:

  • Google Firebase: For cloud hosting, authentication, and database management.
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements. These ads may be personalized based on your device's advertising ID (IDFA/AAID).
  • Google Analytics: To understand how users interact with our app and to improve features.

4. Communication & Notifications

We may send Push Notifications regarding your league status, friend achievements, or app updates. You can manage or disable these notifications through your device settings.

5. Child Privacy (+13 Policy)

LexaRank is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a user under 13 has provided us with personal data, we will delete it immediately.

6. Social Features & Content Moderation

LexaRank allows users to share achievements and badges on their public blog/profile.

  • No Chat/DMs: The Service does not include private messaging, open chat rooms, or user-to-user text communication.
  • Usernames: We monitor usernames to ensure they comply with our community guidelines. Offensive usernames will be restricted or removed.

7. Data Retention & Deletion

We retain your data as long as your account is active. You may request to delete your account and all associated data via the Settings menu in the app or by contacting us at lexarank.team@gmail.com.

8. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can exercise these rights by contacting our team.

9. Contact Us

For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact our Data Protection Officer at:
