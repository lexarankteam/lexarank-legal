LexaRank ("we", "our", or "us") operates the LexaRank mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Service.

When you sign in using Google, Apple, email, or other authentication methods, we may collect:

  • Name

  • Profile photo (if provided by login provider)

b) Guest Accounts

If you use the app without signing in, we may assign an anonymous identifier to store your progress locally or in our systems.

c) Usage & Gameplay Data

We collect gameplay progress, scores, league rankings, achievements, device information, crash reports, and in-app interactions to improve user experience, ensure fair competition, and maintain system security.

d) Purchases and Subscriptions

All payments and subscriptions are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not collect or store credit card or payment details.

We use third-party services such as Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics. These services may collect device identifiers, IP address, advertising identifiers, and usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

We process personal data based on performance of a contract, legitimate interests, and user consent where required for personalized advertising.

3. How We Use Information
