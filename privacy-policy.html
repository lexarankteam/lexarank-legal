Privacy Policy

LAST REVISED: FEBRUARY 13, 2026

At LexaRank, we believe that learning English should be fun and competitive. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data while you practice and compete in our language learning community.

1. Information We Collect

a) Account & Authentication

To keep your progress safe, we support Google, Apple, and Email sign-in. We collect your email address, name, and unique user ID provided by these services. Guest accounts use a locally stored anonymous ID to track progress without a formal account.

b) Learning & Gameplay Activity

To improve your English learning experience, we track your educational interactions:

  • Common mistakes and vocabulary struggles to personalize your lessons.
  • Daily "Streaks", XP (Experience Points), and lesson completion rates.
  • PvP match results, league rankings, and earned badges/achievements.

c) Technical Data & Local Storage

We use Firebase for cloud synchronization and Hive / SharedPreferences for local storage on your device. We collect device identifiers (IDFA/AAID), IP addresses, and crash reports via Google Analytics to ensure system stability and fair play.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Personalization: Using AI to tailor learning content based on your previous mistakes.
  • Competition: Displaying your username and rank on global and weekly Leagues.
  • Security: Detecting bots or cheating to protect the integrity of the leaderboard.
  • Advertising: Showing non-intrusive ads via Google AdMob.

3. Social Features & Safety

LexaRank is built for competition, but we prioritize your safety:

  • No Private Messaging: The Service does not include open chat or DMs. You only share achievements and profile badges.
  • Public Profile: Your username and badges are visible to others. You can change your username once every 30 days.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We share data with trusted partners strictly for operational purposes:

  • Google Firebase: Hosting and Authentication.
  • Google AdMob: Personalized and non-personalized advertising.
  • Crashlytics: Monitoring app performance.

5. Child Privacy (+13 Policy)

LexaRank is strictly for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we discover such data has been collected, it will be deleted immediately.

6. Account Deletion & Data Retention

You own your data. You can delete your account and all associated learning history at any time through Profile > Settings > Delete Account within the app. Alternatively, contact us at lexarank.team@gmail.com for manual removal.

7. Rights & Jurisdiction

We comply with GDPR (EU) and CCPA (California) standards. You have the right to access, correct, or request the deletion of your personal information at any time.
